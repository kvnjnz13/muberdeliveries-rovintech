    Our Story

    Muber is a startup online marketplace that connects buyers of
    hard-to-find items with travelers who can buy and deliver it for
    them.
    Muber is a peer-to-peer package delivery system and
    marketplace that matches the people who want to buy an item
    abroad to the people who can travel, buy, and deliver the item.

    Our Mission

    Muber is not just about creating a social platform, the biggest
    challenge is we have is creating a community that is trustworthy,
    reliable, and secure.

    Our Vision

    Muber provides a way for delivery service fees to be as low as
    possible. It is done by creating a web service that will act as a
    hub for people to connect and interact with one another to buy
    packages and deliver them. It also allows the users to make
    friends along the way during the service.

    MEET MUBER

    Muber is not just a simple delivery platform. It is a growing
    community that allows them to help each other with their tough to get packages.

    Muber community interacts with one another
    differently than the other delivery services. It takes from the trust of each member that makes Muber unique.
